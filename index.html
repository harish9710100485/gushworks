Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

Advanced single-screw extruders deliver consistent melt quality and throughput for uniform wall thickness throughout the pipe length.

  • Temperature-controlled zones
  • Computer-monitored output

Controlled Cooling System

Multi-stage water cooling ensures rapid, uniform temperature reduction preventing warping and maintaining dimensional stability.

  • Graduated cooling chambers
  • Temperature precision monitoring

Accurate Sizing & Calibration

Laser measurement systems and precision calibration sleeves ensure every pipe meets exact dimensional specifications.

  • Laser diameter measurement
  • Automated rejection system

Rigorous Quality Control

Every pipe undergoes hydrostatic testing, dimensional inspection, and material property verification before leaving our facility.

  • 100% hydrostatic testing
  • Third-party certification

Permanent Marking

Inkjet systems apply UV-resistant identification including size, pressure rating, and certification details for full traceability.

  • UV-resistant ink formulations
  • Full traceability codes

Precision Cutting

Automated cutting systems deliver clean, square ends with consistent length accuracy across all pipe sizes.

  • Automated length measurement
  • Burr-free cutting technology

Protective Packaging

Each pipe is carefully packaged to prevent transit damage, with options for bulk supply or retail-ready presentation.

  • UV-protective wrapping
  • Custom labeling available
